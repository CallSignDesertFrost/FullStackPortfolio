I began my Computer Science Fascination in the 6th grade

I grew up with illegal immigrant parents. We were always facing finiancial hardships when I was growing up. My parents worked multiple jobs in order to put food on the table. Neither of my parents had a high school education and yet they did their best to put a roof over our head. I took my education as an escape from my reality. I remember spending the majority of my free time on Myspace.com, where I first learned what it meant to "embed" code into my profile settings. I remembered how satisfying thast was, which motivated me to enroll myself into my first computer science course in the 6th grade. In this course I recreated frogger using an early version of python. After we recreated frogger, we codded a hot dog stand video game where we created objectives and different levels.

Hardships and Struggles

On December 13th, 2009, my mother had received a phone call acknowledging that my father's body had been found after he had been kidnapped. I was 11 years old when he was murdered. Traumatized, I receeded into my studies, receiving the Metro Youth Mayors Award for maintaining a high GPA during challenging times. I stayed dedicated to my frogger game, altering it and updating it as time went on.

High School

My time in high school was challenging, my family was torn apart, my older brother had gotten deported and I lived in fear of loosing my mother to deportation and becoming orphaned as a teenager. To hide from these daily worries, I dove deeper into web development. I enrolled an entire semester to my schools Web Development course where I learned to use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on Adobe Dreamweaver and later publishing and hosting on Weebly. Using Web Development as a resource, I was able to attain a Summer internship with the Denver Museum of Nature and Science where I worked alongside the Museum Curators in Chaco Canyon, NM excavating pre historic turtles out from the middle of the desert. I later reconstructed the Morondova Basin in Madagascar during the Cretaceous time period. It was at this time that I had decided that a work place with AC would be more ideal.

US Navy Submarine Qualified

After graduating high school, I enlisted in the US Navy's Submarine Volunteer Program. During my time in the service I was able to work on fast tracking my mothers residency paper work and was finally able to keep my mother from facing deportation. During my time in the Navy I also received a Secret Clearence that allowed me to work on board near a Nuclear Fission Reactor. During these busy days I maintained my skills through platforms like CodeAcademy.com

Pursuit of Bachelors in Computer Science
Franklin University Class of 2027!

After being medically discharged with CPTSD, Insomnia, the pandemic hit, I chose to dedicate my time to my family, myself, and my friends.
After a few years of re-adjusting to society I took the time to pursue Bachelors in Computer Science from Franklin University and am currently set to Graduate in the summer of 2027.
